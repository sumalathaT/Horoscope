    Pros of the Gemini Sagittarius Relationship:
      Both Gemini and Sagittarius are Mutable signs. Hence, they are extremely adaptive to their surroundings and do not take long to change their ways according to the requirements of the situation. Gemini is an Air sign while Sagittarius is a Fire sign. If they get their dynamic right, the winds will blow into the blazing fire, which will thereby help both the elements grow in power. They are both passionate beings and will love each other’s company. The best thing about this relationship is its spontaneity. Gemini man and Sagittarius woman and vice-versa both the partners will not know what they might do next and that adds a riveting spunk to their bond. They will never bore each other in this relationship and not a moment that they spend together will ever be uninteresting or mundane.

    Cons of the Gemini Sagittarius Relationship:
      The problem with this kind of relationship is that it may never cross the fun stage and will keep fluctuating between high energy points, without settling for a deeper connection anywhere along the way. Hence, even after dating each other for quiet some time, Gemini and Sagittarius might think that they do not really know each other all that well. Moreover, the unpredictability in their personalities can make this relationship too volatile at times. There is no stability or security in the equation, which will cause problems for them in the long run. However, if they decide to make it work with each other, they can form an unbreakable bond that will transcend all the boundaries of time. A Sagittarius and a Gemini committed to each other will most probably end up in a blissfully satisfying, beautifully enriching, and completely thrilling relationship.
